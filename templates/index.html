Keep track of your movments and help in the fight against the Covid-19

Welcome to Covid-19 Diary!

Covid-19 Diary is a web application that allows users to keep track of their movements in zones where the Covid-19 is highly active. The application enables the user to write down all the locations where he/she has been in the last days and all the people he/she has seen as well date and hour.

The Covid-19 Diary will provide a complete diary, where you will be able to follow all your movements and help in the fight against the Corona virus. The idea is simple: if you are infected, you can get in touch with the people you have had contact with and the places you have been to inform them that you are infected. On the other hand, if, eventually, you know that someone with whom you have recently been infected or that one of the places where you have been has been found to have a case of Covid-19, you can take precautions as if you were infected. The idea is exactly to help us to prevent the spread of the virus through simple actions that depend only on ourselves, without governments or companies watching us.

Instructions

To use it, you need to first create an account with a valid email. The register is necessary to help us protect all your data on our database. All the information provided by the user is encrypted, like that no one, not even us, can have access to the information you write down in your diary.

After you created an account, you can access your diary and start writting. You can also get the latest information and statistics about the Covid-19 in your country. We highly recommend the users to check this area frequently, to always be aware of what's going on in their country. All the information is provided and updated in real time by Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center
